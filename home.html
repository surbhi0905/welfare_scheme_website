Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G)

PM Housing Scheme – Rural
Housing
About

Provides financial assistance to homeless or kachcha-house families in rural India to build a pucca (permanent) house with basic amenities.

Benefits
₹1.20 Lakh (Plains) ₹1.30 Lakh (Hills/NE) 90 days MGNREGA wages
Eligibility
  • Homeless or living in 0/1-room kachcha house
  • Listed in SECC 2011 data (Socio Economic Caste Census)
  • No pucca house in name of any family member
  • BPL (Below Poverty Line) family priority
  • SC/ST, minorities, freed bonded labourers get priority

MGNREGS – Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee

100 Days Guaranteed Work
Employment
About

Legal guarantee of 100 days of wage employment per year to every rural household whose adult members are willing to do unskilled manual work.

Benefits
100 days/year guaranteed work Daily wage ₹220–₹374 (state-wise) Unemployment allowance if no work given
Eligibility
  • Any adult member of a rural household
  • Willing to do unskilled manual labour
  • Must register with the local Gram Panchayat
  • Work must be provided within 15 days of application
  • At least 1/3 beneficiaries must be women

PM-KISAN – Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

Direct Income Support to Farmers
Farming
About

Provides direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmer families, paid in three equal instalments of ₹2,000 directly into their bank accounts.

Benefits
₹6,000/year (₹2,000 × 3 instalments) Direct Bank Transfer (DBT)
Eligibility
  • Small and marginal farmers (land up to 2 hectares)
  • Name must be in land records as farmer
  • Must have Aadhaar-linked bank account
  • Excluded: income tax payers, institutional land holders, govt employees (except Class IV)
  • Retired pensioners earning above ₹10,000/month ineligible

Ayushman Bharat – PM Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

₹5 Lakh Free Health Insurance
Health
About

World's largest health insurance scheme providing free hospitalisation coverage of ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care at empanelled public and private hospitals.

Benefits
₹5 Lakh/year insurance 1500+ treatments covered Cashless treatment at 25,000+ hospitals
Eligibility
  • Rural: families with kachcha walls/roof, no adult member, female-headed households
  • Manual scavenging families, primitive tribal groups, bonded labourers
  • Based on SECC 2011 database — check name at official portal
  • No cap on family size or age

Crop Insurance for Farmers
Farming
About

Provides financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage due to unforeseen events like floods, drought, pests, diseases or natural calamities.

Benefits

Safe Motherhood & Cash for Delivery
Health
About

Promotes institutional delivery among poor pregnant women by providing cash assistance to both the mother and the ASHA worker to reduce maternal and neonatal mortality.

Benefits

National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP)

Pension for Elderly, Widows & Disabled
Pension
About

Umbrella scheme providing monthly pensions to old-age persons, widows, and disabled persons belonging to BPL households across rural and urban India.

Benefits
₹200–₹500/month (central share) State adds additional top-up Lump-sum for family on BPL death
Eligibility
  • Indira Gandhi Old Age Pension (IGNOAP): Age 60+, BPL household
  • Indira Gandhi Widow Pension (IGNWPS): Widow aged 40–79, BPL
  • Indira Gandhi Disability Pension (IGNDPS): 80% or more disability, 18+ years, BPL
  • National Family Benefit Scheme: Death of primary breadwinner (18–59) in BPL family
  • Apply at Block Development Office / Gram Panchayat
